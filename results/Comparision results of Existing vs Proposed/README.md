## Comparison of Existing vs Proposed Model

This folder contains graphical comparisons of performance metrics between CNN, GAN, and the proposed Hybrid CNN–Transformer model.

### 📊 Metrics Comparison

- **SSIM (Structural Similarity Index):**  
  The proposed model achieves higher SSIM, indicating better preservation of structural information.

- **PSNR (Peak Signal-to-Noise Ratio):**  
  Higher PSNR values in the proposed model show improved image quality and less distortion.

- **RMSE (Root Mean Square Error):**  
  Lower RMSE in the proposed model indicates more accurate image reconstruction.

- **Mutual Information (MI):**  
  Higher MI values suggest better information fusion from CT and MRI images.

- **Entropy:**  
  Increased entropy reflects richer information content in the fused image.

### ✅ Overall Analysis

The proposed Hybrid CNN–Transformer model outperforms CNN and GAN models across most evaluation metrics, providing better visual quality and more reliable fusion results.
