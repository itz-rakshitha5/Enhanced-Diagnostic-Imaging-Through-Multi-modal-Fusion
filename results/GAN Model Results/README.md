
## GAN Model Results

### 📷 Qualitative Results
The GAN-based model generates visually enhanced fused images with better texture representation.  
However, it may introduce slight artifacts and inconsistencies in some regions.

### 📊 Quantitative Results
The quantitative evaluation includes metrics such as **PSNR, SSIM, Mutual Information (MI), RMSE, and Entropy**.  
The GAN model shows improved performance compared to CNN, but may have unstable metric values due to adversarial training.

### ✅ Conclusion
GAN improves visual quality and detail representation compared to CNN, but lacks stability and consistency in fusion performance.
